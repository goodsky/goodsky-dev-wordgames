## Notes
- Games use a mix of emojis and text words for variety
- Color-focused games with colored text: k3 (Red/Yellow/Blue/Green), k13 (Orange/Pink/Purple/Brown), k19 (Color Words)
- Several games include word-based categories for learning: k4 Animal Sounds, k7 Shape Words, k8 Body Words, k9 Feeling Words, k10 In the Sky, k11 Circle Things, k12 Favorite Foods, k16 Things That Are Hot, k17 Rhymes with CAT, k18 Number Words, k19 Color Words, k20 Starts with S
- ABC Phonics series (all words have altText like "Apple starts with A"): k21 (A/B/C/D), k22 (E/F/G/H), k23 (J/K/L/M), k24 (N/O/P/R), k25 (S/T/W/Z)
- Categories have 4-15 items depending on how many strong matches exist
- Difficulty levels progress from very simple (0) to slightly more abstract (3)
- More items = more replayability since 4 random items are selected each game
